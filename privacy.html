Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Tiger Gas ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website tigergas.co.uk or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact details (when you call us or request a quote)
  • Address and property details (required to provide our services)
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type (collected automatically via server logs)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide gas, heating and plumbing services
  • To respond to your enquiries and arrange appointments
  • To comply with Gas Safe and other regulatory requirements
  • To improve our website and services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information with regulatory bodies (such as Gas Safe Register) where required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law and regulatory obligations.

6. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. Essential cookies may be used to ensure the website functions correctly.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please call us on 07999 943 640.
