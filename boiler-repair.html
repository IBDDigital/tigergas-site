Common Boiler Issues

Our engineers are experienced in diagnosing and repairing all common boiler faults.

🌡 Broken Thermostat

Thermostat not responding or reading incorrect temperatures, causing heating to stay on or off.

💧 Leaking Pipes

Water leaks from pipework, joints or the boiler itself. Can cause water damage if left untreated.

🔥 Kettling Noises

Rumbling or banging sounds caused by limescale build-up or sludge restricting water flow.

💧 No Hot Water

Boiler running but no hot water. Could be a diverter valve, thermostat or pump issue.

🔧 Faulty Pump or Valve

Central heating pump not circulating water or valves stuck, causing cold radiators.

⚡ Ignition Failure

Boiler failing to ignite. Could be a faulty electrode, gas valve or PCB issue.
