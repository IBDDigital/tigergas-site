What Our Engineers Check

During a boiler service, our engineers carry out a comprehensive inspection covering:

  • Gas pressure
  • Flue condition and integrity
  • Electrical connections
  • Fan operation
  • Seals and gaskets
  • Safety devices
  • Condensate trap and pipe
  • Pipework and connections

Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to service your boiler is in Autumn or Spring. This ensures your boiler is ready for the colder months and avoids the busy winter period.

A boiler service typically takes up to 1 hour to complete.

A well-maintained boiler can last between 10 to 15 years. Regular servicing helps extend its lifespan and keeps it running efficiently.
