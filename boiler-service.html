Boiler Servicing

Keep your boiler running safely and efficiently with a professional annual service from Tiger Gas.

Why Service Your Boiler?

Annual servicing is the minimum requirement to preserve your boiler warranty. A well-maintained boiler runs more efficiently, lasts longer, and keeps your home safe.

We recommend servicing in Autumn or Spring so your boiler is ready for the colder months. A typical service takes up to one hour.

  • Maintains manufacturer warranty
  • Reduces breakdown risk
  • Lowers energy bills
  • Keeps your boiler at peak performance
  • Identifies faults early
  • Annual service reminder (if opted in)

What Our Engineers Check

During a boiler service, our engineers carry out a comprehensive inspection covering:

  • Gas pressure and flow rate
  • Flue combustion analysis
  • Electrical connections and wiring
  • Fan operation and speed
  • Seals, gaskets and o-rings
  • Electrodes and ignition
  • Safety devices and controls
  • Condensate trap and pipe
