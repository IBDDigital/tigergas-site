What is an ASHP?

An air source heat pump absorbs heat from outside air to heat your home and hot water. They work efficiently even when outdoor temperatures are low, making them ideal for the North East climate.

We are able to install, service and repair air source heat pumps. Our engineers are fully trained and qualified to work on ASHP systems.
