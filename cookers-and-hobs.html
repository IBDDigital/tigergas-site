Supply Pipe Upgrades

Older properties may require pipework upgrades to meet current gas safety regulations.

Modern gas appliances often require updated supply pipes to comply with current regulations. If your property has older pipework, it may need upgrading before a new cooker or hob can be safely installed.

Our engineer will assess your existing pipework during the installation visit. If an upgrade is needed, we will explain exactly what is required and provide a clear quote before any work begins.
